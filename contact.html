About Our Organization

CSEquityAI is a forward-thinking organization at the intersection of artificial intelligence and social equity. Our team of researchers, educators, and technologists is dedicated to using AI to identify and reduce disparities in computer science education and professional advancement.

At CSEquityAI, we leverage cutting-edge artificial intelligence to drive meaningful change, promote inclusive innovation, and empower the next generation of tech leaders.

Our Story

Born out of a passion for equity and a deep belief in the transformative power of technology, CSEquityAI was founded to ensure that the future of AI includes everyone. We use data-driven tools, community partnerships, and ethical AI practices to make systemic change possible.
